❮ ❯

A drivable EVTOL long-range airplane

The Cloudranger rides on the ground as a three-wheel motorcycle. It takes off and lands vertically like a drone, and flies to the destination like an airplane.

It uses four electric lifting propellers for takeoff and landing, while being propelled during flight by a gas motor. It drives on the ground using electric power.

Control is done by wire with the help of a flight controller, which facilitates completely autonomic operation, if needed.

It can be registered for the driving on the ground as a low-speed motorcycle (LSM).

Technical specifications (preliminary)

Load capacity: 100 kg

Gross weight: 300 kg

Wing loading: 45 kg/m2

Flight time: 3 hours

Lift motors: 4 x 25 kW

Drive motor: 60 HP

Battery capacity: 5 kWh

Fuel capacity: 40 litres (10 gallons)

Flight speed: 250 km/hour

Ground speed: 70 km/hour

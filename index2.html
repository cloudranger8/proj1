About CloudRanger

CloudRanger is a concept-stage personal electric vehicle that merges ground transportation with vertical takeoff and landing (VTOL) capabilities...

  • Expandable wings that fold during ground mode and extend in flight
  • Electric hub motors for road propulsion
  • A rear-mounted pusher propeller for forward flight
  • Four VTOL propellers for vertical lift and hover stability

Vision

CloudRanger is built for a future where personal aerial mobility is not a luxury — but a practical, scalable solution...

Technical Overview

CloudRanger is a dual-mode electric vehicle engineered for both ground travel and vertical flight...

  • Drivable chassis: Equipped with electric hub motors
  • VTOL lift system: Four vertical propellers
  • Forward flight: A rear-mounted pusher propeller
  • Expandable wings: Fold and extend automatically
  • Flight control: Autonomous or semi-autonomous
  • Power system: Modular battery packs
  • Frame: Lightweight composite structure

Modular Architecture

CloudRanger is designed with modularity at its core — enabling rapid iteration, simplified maintenance, and scalable deployment...

  • Propulsion modules: VTOL and ground motors in pods
  • Wing assembly: Foldable wings with pivot mounts
  • Battery packs: Hot-swappable and configurable
  • Control systems: Redundant flight and drive controllers
  • Sensor suite: Modular mounts for cameras, LIDAR
  • Chassis frame: Lightweight skeleton with mounting points
